Fractals: The Hidden Dimention

Fractals are analog patterns of the life and world around us. Even though fractals just look like pretty designs, they are some of the most powerful concepts that we use in every field of science. Our most powerful artificial intelligence neural networks use fractal equations to recognize objects that stand out in a visual field. Biologists find fractal patterns throughout all organisms which suggests that these patterns reflect much deeper laws of energy and our world.

Signal generators and sound waves

Sound waves are vibrations, an ebb and flow of a medium (usually air) and can be represented in a graph. Signal generators are able to create electrical fluctuations from graphs, so if you create signals that have the frequency of a musical note and put them through a speaker, the speaker will play the note. Coordinating my classmates to play the other notes to form chords to the song Umbrella by Rihanna was one of the more fun classes I had in 2010.

Ubuntu Phone

This device will revolutionize how we look at phones and computers but not this year. The Ubuntu phone has a lot of promise and on paper but all the reviews say that it needs more work. The main feature that makes this phone stand apart is that you can dock it in any computer and use the phone as its own computer. You could go to potentially any computer and run the linux operating system with all the files and programs from your phone and use the power and speed of the computer. Maybe next year it will be all it has been promised to be.
